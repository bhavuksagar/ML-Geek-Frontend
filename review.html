Review Analyser

Summary

Review analyser helps to understand the sentiment of the review. Amazon used this program on their website to analyse the reveiws of other products. It classify the review into 3 category Positve, Negative and Neutral. It is a NLP(Natural Language Processing) problem and accuracy of the model is 90%. For training we used Airline-Reviews-Dataset which contains thousands of reviews of all category. Program convert the text into numerical form for performing the further processing.
